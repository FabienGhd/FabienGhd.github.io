About Me...

Hi there! 👋 My name is Fabien, and I'm a first-year graduate student studying cybersecurity at the University of Rennes.

What I like most about writing code is that it combines a lot of different aspects of my personality. I like rational thoughts but on the other side I also really enjoy the collaborative and creative mindset we can have when building something from scratch. As a curious student, my main areas of interest lie in CS theory and cybersecurity.

What draws me to cybersecurity is the opportunity to make a positive impact by protecting people's digital assets and privacy. It's a field that requires both technical expertise and critical thinking skills, and I find the challenge of identifying and mitigating potential cyber threats extremely rewarding. Whether it's through implementing stronger security measures or educating others about safe online practices, I believe that cybersecurity has the power to make a significant difference in today's interconnected world.
